Galaxian by Unity

Description

This is an assignment for CS 376 class (Game Design and Develpment). I used Unity to build this game from scratch.

Instruction

Use keyword's left and right arrow to control the player ship, and use the space key to shoot bullet. You can shoot at most 1 bullet per second. The goal is to shoot 10 enemies. You lose your life when an enemy get pass over you, and if you lose 3 lifes, you lose.
